About

I am an endearing designer located in Richmond, British Columbia where I look forward to all the possible areas I can display my array of skills. From graphical works such as logos and business cards to architectural drafting with CAD, what I bring to any community is my broad skillset and a critical eye for detail and finesse. Having previously worked at an architectural office, I can provide the experience of a team player and bring my ambition as a young professional.

Currently, I have graduated from the University of British Columbia with a bachelors in Environmental Design (B.Ends). Experienced in both 2D drafting and 3D modelling software such as AutoCAD and Rhino/Revit, I also make use of proficiency in the Adobe Creative Suite to make my projects come to life. As a quick learner, I am willing to embrace new challenges and strive to adapt to any situation that come my way.
